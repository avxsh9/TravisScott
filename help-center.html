Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.), and Net Banking from all major Indian banks.

How do I receive my e-tickets after purchase?

Your e-tickets are sent to your registered email address instantly after payment confirmation. You can also find them in the "My Tickets" section of your account.

What is the fee for selling tickets?

Listing your tickets is completely free. We charge a small 3% commission fee only when your tickets sell successfully.

When will I get paid for the tickets I sell?

Payments are processed and transferred to your bank account within 24 hours after the event has successfully concluded.

What happens if an event is cancelled?

If an event is cancelled, buyers receive a full refund automatically. As a seller, your listing will be cancelled, and no fees will be charged.

How can I reset my password?

On the Sign In page, click the "Forgot Password?" link. Enter your email, and we'll send you instructions to reset it.
