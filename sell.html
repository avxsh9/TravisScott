Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to sell?

Listing is completely free. We only charge a 0% fee when your tickets sell (for now!).

When do I get paid?

Payment is transferred to your bank account within 24 hours of the ticket sale being completed.

What if my event gets cancelled?

If an event is cancelled, we automatically process refunds for buyers and remove your listing. No fees apply.

Can I change my price after listing?

Yes, you can update your ticket prices anytime before they sell through your seller dashboard.

How do I transfer mobile tickets?

For mobile tickets, we provide a secure transfer process through the original ticketing platform.

Is it safe to sell on TicketAdda?

Yes, we verify all buyers and provide seller protection. Payments are processed securely through our platform.
