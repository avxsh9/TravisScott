Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At TicketAdda, we are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it. This Privacy Policy applies to all services offered by TicketAdda Technologies Pvt Ltd.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use TicketAdda, we may collect personal information such as:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID for seller verification.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, UPI IDs, billing address.
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history, ticket listings, and sales records.

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our services:

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent on site, click patterns.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to facilitate ticket buying and selling, process payments, verify identities, provide customer support, and improve our platform.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with other users to complete a transaction, with trusted third-party service providers (like payment processors), or when required by law.
